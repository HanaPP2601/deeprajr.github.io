Acts of Time

In times of seeking ourselves, we arrive at a point at which it feels like every piece of sand is turning against us, which may or may not be with a cause. It feels like a total numbness and something carrying us to the mere infinity of emptiness.

All we have to do is take a deep breath, a breath of acceptance, a breath of self-love, a breath of peace, a breath of freedom of feelings.

And never let that breath choke you.

The mystic future awaits us with a bag of gifts of mysteries.
